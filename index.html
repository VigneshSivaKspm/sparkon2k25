About

Welcome to Sparkon 2025, a state-level technical symposium proudly organized by the Circuit Department of Shree Venkateshwara Hi-Tech Polytechnic College. organized exclusively for diploma students, this event aims to :

  • Encourage students,
  • Inspire learning,
  • Foster collaboration.

Shree Venkateshwara Hi-Tech Polytechnic College, a Seat of Wisdom, is one of the foremost institutions, gifted with a cheerful atmosphere, located at Othakuthirai. The college was established in the year 2009 by Shree Venkateshwara Educational & Charitable Trust. SVHPC has been fortunate to have the guidance of illustrious managing trustees and Principal with foresight and far-reaching vision. SVHPC has been in the forefront by innovation in technical education.

We prepare the students to develop professionalism and censorious thoughts in them. The college offers a haven of peace and tranquil atmosphere for students who have the urge to learn and master. As per the prologue “Survival of the Fittest One”, we earnestly prepare the students to be the fittest in modern society. Discipline and Perfection are the watchwords of our Institution.
